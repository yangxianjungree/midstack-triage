# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Midstack Triage to be a practical, respectful project for people improving production troubleshooting systems. Participants are expected to communicate with patience, assume good intent where reasonable, and keep discussions focused on evidence, design, and implementation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep issues and pull requests actionable.
- Use inclusive language.
- Accept constructive disagreement.
- Redact customer data, credentials, private infrastructure details, and incident artifacts before sharing.
- Respect the maintainer capacity and best-effort support policy in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, logs, customer data, or exploit details.
- Repeatedly demanding urgent production support through public issues.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove content, close issues, decline pull requests, or block participants when behavior violates this code of conduct or creates safety risk.

Security issues must follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not disclose vulnerabilities or sensitive incident material in public discussions.
